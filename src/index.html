What I do

AI Systems for Data, Compliance, and Climate

I build systems that help people make sense of data. That usually means machine learning, automation, or working with spatial and environmental info. I focus on tools that get used and not just models that sit in notebooks.

  • I design and build ML systems from first prototype to production, including data pipelines, APIs, and automation. I can work with PyTorch, APIs, and Ops, etc. to make AI stuffs actually usable.

  • I worked on systems that track, monitor, model, or report on climate data, often using spatial pipelines or computer vision. That includes tools for Scope 3, ESG, or compliance cases.

  • I’ve worked with embedded systems, high-alt balloons, and vision-based monitors. I build quick prototypes that connect sensors, firmware, and ML logic, good for testing real-world ideas fast.

Process

How I Work

I like building useful things. Most of the work starts with data, but the goal is always a system that solves something real for business, climate, or just making life easier.

  • 01

    Discovery

    First step is to figure out what the system really has to do, and why; and talk to whoever knows the domain best, not just staring at the data.

  • 02

    Design

    Next is to sketch out a system that fits, technically and practically. No matter if it’s automation or data workflows, I choose tools based on fit - I'm too old for trends :) .

  • 03

    Build & Iterate

    I build in steps. Each part must be tested, with room to change direction. I keep things clean and share progress early and often.

  • 04

    Deliver & Support

    I write things down and keep handovers simple. If you need support later — fixing bugs, adding features, I'll stick around.
